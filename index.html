Historic Figures

Leonardo da Vinci

Learn about the renaissance man.

Leonardo da Vinci was an Italian polymath who lived during the Renaissance. He is widely recognized for his contributions to art, science, and engineering.

Cleopatra

Explore the life of the last Pharaoh of Egypt.

Cleopatra VII Philopator was the last active ruler of the Ptolemaic Kingdom of Egypt, known for her political acumen and romantic liaisons with prominent Roman figures.

Literature Overview

Don Quixote

Discover the story of the self-styled knight.

Don Quixote, written by Miguel de Cervantes, is a novel about a man who becomes obsessed with the idea of chivalry and sets out to revive it.

Pride and Prejudice

Explore Jane Austen's classic novel.

Pride and Prejudice, by Jane Austen, is a romantic novel that deals with issues of class, marriage, and morality in 19th century England.
